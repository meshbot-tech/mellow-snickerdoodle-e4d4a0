Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — Compliant with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

1. Who We Are

LumiCore is a strategy-led digital consultancy based in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide CRM optimization, web design, SEO, branding, digital marketing, and content strategy services. Our contact details: hello@lumicore.co.ke | +254 713082563

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you contact us or submit our inquiry form, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business name and industry
  • Project requirements, budget, and timeline
  • Website usage data through Google Analytics (with your consent)
3. How We Use Your Information
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our services
  • To send project updates and communications
  • To improve our website and services using anonymized analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations under Kenyan law
4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure our website works correctly, and optional analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. You can choose which cookies to accept via our cookie banner. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the website to function.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers including Formspree (form processing), Google Analytics (site analytics), and Zoho Mail (email communications). These providers are contractually required to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you request deletion of your data, we will action this within 30 days unless we are legally required to retain it.

7. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner
8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries or to exercise your rights, contact us at: hello@lumicore.co.ke or WhatsApp +254 713082563.
