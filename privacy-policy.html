Who We Are

For the purposes of the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, LumiCore is the Data Controller of your personal information.

  • Website: lumicore.co.ke
  • Email: hello@lumicore.co.ke
  • Location: Nairobi, Kenya
02

What Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Information you provide directly:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Business or organization name
  • Details of your enquiry or business challenge
  • Messages sent through our AI chatbot

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as national ID numbers, financial information, health data, or biometric data.

03

How We Collect Your Information

We collect your personal information through the following channels:

  • Contact Form: When you fill in the enquiry form on our website
  • Google Form: When you complete our project intake or discovery call booking form (hosted by Google)
  • AI Chatbot: When you interact with our LumiCore AI Assistant on our website, powered by Anthropic's Claude AI
  • WhatsApp: When you initiate contact via our WhatsApp button
  • Analytics: Automatically through Google Analytics when you browse our website
  • Email: When you contact us directly at hello@lumicore.co.ke
04

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following lawful purposes under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019:

  • To respond to your enquiries — when you contact us, we use your details to get back to you
  • To provide our services — to deliver the consulting, design, or strategy services you have engaged us for
  • To schedule consultations — to book and manage discovery calls and project meetings
  • To improve our website — using analytics data to understand how visitors use our site and improve user experience
  • To send relevant updates — with your consent, to share information about our services, insights, or offers that may interest you
  • To comply with legal obligations — to meet our obligations under Kenyan law

We will never use your personal information for automated decision-making that produces legal or significant effects without your explicit consent.

05

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools we use to operate our business (Google Analytics, Google Forms, Anthropic Claude AI, WhatsApp Business) — only to the extent necessary to provide our services
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or a government authority in Kenya
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred — you will be notified in advance
  • With your consent: In any other circumstances, only with your explicit permission

Third-party processors we use:

  • Google LLC (Analytics, Forms, Workspace) — USA, governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic PBC (Claude AI chatbot) — USA, governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Meta Platforms (WhatsApp Business) — USA, governed by WhatsApp's Privacy Policy
06

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes it was collected:

  • Enquiry and contact form data: 2 years from last contact
  • Client project data: 5 years from project completion (for legal and financial record-keeping)
  • AI chatbot conversations: 90 days, then permanently deleted
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default retention)
  • Email correspondence: 3 years from last contact

After these periods, your data is securely deleted or anonymized. You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Your Rights below).

07

Your Rights Under Kenyan Law

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to be informed — to know how your data is collected and used (this policy)
  • Right of access — to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — to request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — to object to processing of your data for direct marketing
  • Right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@lumicore.co.ke. We will respond within 30 days as required by the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. Your request is free of charge.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya (ODPC) at odpc.go.ke.

08

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience and understand how our site is used.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand visitor behaviour (pages visited, time on site, traffic sources). These are anonymized.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, accept essential cookies only, or manage your preferences at any time.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.
