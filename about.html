✦ Our Story

Where LumiCore came from.

LumiCore was born from a simple frustration: too many businesses in Kenya were investing in digital services that didn't connect. A website here. A social media campaign there. A CRM no one actually used. The pieces existed — but no one was building the picture.

We started LumiCore to fix that. Our belief is that digital strategy works best when every element — your CRM, website, SEO, brand, marketing, and content — is designed to amplify the others. Not six separate vendors. One connected ecosystem.

Based in Nairobi, we work with startups who need to launch right, growing businesses who've hit a plateau, and organizations who want to communicate their mission more effectively in a digital-first world.

We're not a large agency with layers of account managers. We're a focused, senior-level team that gets deep into your business, thinks strategically, and delivers work that actually moves the needle. Clarity. Systems. Growth. That's not just our tagline — it's exactly what we do, in that order, every time.
